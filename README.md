## 🚀 The Problem
Most students managing money on their own for the first time run dry before the month ends. The budgeting apps that exist were built for salaried adults and do not map well to how students actually spend: canteen meals, auto fares, last-minute stationery, weekend plans. They end up guessing where the money went and running out of funds before the month is over.

## 💡 Our Solution
*EXPENZA* tracks cash where it counts. We discard traditional boring accounting for an aggressive, futuristic glassmorphic UI. 

**Key Features:**
*   **Predictive AI "Days Until Broke" Math**: We calculate your true "monthly burn rate" by isolating your non-negotiable fixed expenses (Autopay) from your manual variable spending (Food, Outings). The app accurately predicts exactly how many days of cash you have left.
*   **The Keeper Stash**: A dedicated, dynamic savings jar. Lock your savings away instantly; the UI deducts it from your main budget immediately to enforce strict financial discipline.
*   **Smart Categorization**: Log exactly how you spent it with emojis, UPI/Cash/Card tags, and date tracking.
*   **Spotify-Style Month Wrap**: A cinematic recap of your monthly spending habits that categorizes you as a *Beginner, Median, or Hard-core Saver*.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Setup
This project was built entirely natively for lightning-fast performance. No heavy frameworks.
- **HTML5** & **CSS3** (Glassmorphism & Advanced Keyframe Animations)
- **Vanilla JavaScript** (ES6+)
- **Chart.js** (Data Visualization)
- **Local Storage API** (Secure Offline Persistence)

### To Run Locally:
1. Clone the repository.
2. Open terminal in the project directory.
3. Serve it using Python: `python3 -m http.server 8000`
4. Visit `localhost:8000` in your browser.
